Live — Donation Drive
Every Drop Counts

One donation.Three lives.Your blood can save a mother, a child, a stranger tonight.

Every 2 seconds, someone in India needs blood. Hospitals run short every single day. You have the power to change that — in just one hour.

Why It Matters

Blood cannot be
manufactured.

It can only come from generous people like you. No lab, no machine — just human kindness.

Save Up to 3 Lives

A single donation is split into red cells, platelets, and plasma — three separate gifts of life.

Takes Only 45 Minutes

The actual donation process takes less than 10 minutes.

Safe & Medically Supervised

Every donation is performed by trained medical staff.

Someone Always Needs It

Cancer patients, accident victims, mothers during childbirth.

Your Body Replenishes It

Your body fully replaces donated blood within 24-48 hours.

Become a Community Hero

Regular donors are the backbone of our healthcare system.
